## Expected Output Types

For "Coke Zero":
  → Shows cheapest supermarkets, overall winner, $100 quantity

For "milk":
  → Lists all milk products, highlights cheapest, gives $100 calculation

For "cheese":
  → Shows cheese items, picks cheapest, may not be actual cheese (e.g., nacho cheese)

## Notes

- The skill searches Product Name, Brand, and Categories.
- If nothing matches, it says "Sorry, I couldn't find any products matching..."
- Prices are HK dollars ($).
- $100 quantity assumes you buy the cheapest item repeatedly.
- Supermarket codes: WELLCOME, PARKNSHOP, AEON, JASONS, etc.
